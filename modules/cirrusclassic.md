### Outputs

*   **OUT L** - left output of the stereo signal
*   **OUT R** - right output of the stereo signal
*   **BUS Ctrl** - Midi CC20 CV from Master I/O module
*   **BUS CV** - MIDI pitch signal from the Master I/O MIDI to CV converter
